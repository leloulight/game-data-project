CounterSpy is a side-scrolling stealth video game developed by Dynamighty.[2] It was published by Sony Computer Entertainment as a cross-buy and cross-save title for the PlayStation 3, PlayStation 4, and PlayStation Vita.[3] Subseqeuently, the game was released on Android and iOS on September 5, 2014.

The games received a mixture of average and positive reviews. It was mostly praised for the art and soundtrack of the game. According to the IGN review, the "Inconsistent AI" proved to be a noticeable issue for the game.

Gameplay[edit]

The game is played as a 2D sidescroller with stealth elements.[4]

Story[edit]

The game takes place during an alternate history Cold War.[5]

Development[edit]

The game is being developed with assistance from SCE Foster City Studio, which has previously worked on games in the Uncharted, Infamous, and Resistance series of video games.[6] While historically known for collaborating with the series' respective large development studios, CounterSpy marks the first time they've teamed up with an indie development team, "Dynamighty".[6] The team, while classified as "indie", comprises past staff from LucasArts and Pixar.[7]
