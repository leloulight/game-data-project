Fantasy Life (ファンタジーライフ, Fantajī Raifu?) is a role-playing video game developed by Level-5, with assistance from 1-UP Studio and h.a.n.d., for the Nintendo 3DS. The game was previously being developed for the Nintendo DS.[3] On April 10, 2013, Level-5 revealed that the game had sold over 300,000 copies in Japan since its launch.[4]

An expansion for the 3DS, titled Fantasy Life Link!, was released in Japanese markets on July 25, 2013.[5]
