Rekoil is a multiplayer first person shooter developed by Plastic Piranha. It features downtrodden "Minutemen" pitted against their oppressors, Darkwater Inc, in a world where the only goal is to survive the unrelenting pandemic that has swept across the globe.[1]

Gameplay[edit]

Rekoil's gameplay consists of infantry combat that is class-based, meaning players choose from a number of different specialized roles.

It will be possible to set up custom servers. The game also offers support for modding.

On 1 October 2012, a closed beta for Rekoil was launched.[2] The game is also on the Steam Greenlight Program.

Features[edit]

  • 10 maps on launch
  • Over 40 weapons
  • 10 game types currently under development
  • 5 customizable classes
  • Highly customizable server setups and gameplay
  • Dedicated servers & public server files
  • Full modification support and promotion from Plastic Piranha
