Crafting Mama (クラフトママ Kurafuto Mama) is a crafting simulation-styled minigame compilation video game developed by Cooking Mama Limited that was released on Nintendo DS during October 2010. It is a spin-off from the Cooking Mama popular series.[1]

Gameplay[edit]

As the previous versions of the Cooking Mama series, this game focus on mini-games. The player chooses an object to craft, which is done through a variable number of mini-games. Crafting Mama introduces the "crafting games", where you can play a mini-game related to the crafted piece. The objects crafted by the player can be used in the customization of the Mama and some of the elements on the game's GUI.

Reception[edit]

IGN gave the game an overall score of 6 out of 10, claiming that some of the mini-games "don't work at all" and that it is "not that fun".[1]
