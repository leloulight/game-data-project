Age of Hammer Wars is an Action / Arcade game created by Dynamic Systems and was published by iSquared Games. The game was released in Europe and Asia with PSP Go launch on April 1, 2010. It was later released in the North America region for PSP Go on October 19, 2010.[1]

Gameplay[edit]

There are two modes of play: Campaign and Free Missions. Campaign is basically a "campaign mode", where the levels are played in order, while in Free Missions, the goal is to beat selected levels as fast as possible.
