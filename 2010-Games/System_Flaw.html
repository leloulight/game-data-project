System Flaw (known as System Flaw Recruit as a DSiWare title) is an action shooter video game released exclusively for the Nintendo DSi on October 27, 2009 in the United States and for DSiWare in North America on April 26, 2010 and Europe on April 16, 2010.[2][3] This is the first time for the first Nintendo DSi-exclusively game. The game, using the DSi cameras to take video, turns a player's surroundings into the playing environment.[2]
