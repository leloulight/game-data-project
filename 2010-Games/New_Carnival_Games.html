New Carnival Games is a party video game developed by Cat Daddy Games released on September 21, 2010 for the Wii and Nintendo DS.[1] The game was published by 2K Play and is a sequel to Carnival Games and Carnival Games: Mini-Golf.[1]

Features[edit]

The game features over 30 minigames. It can support the Wii MotionPlus accessory, but it is not really required to play the game. For the Wii, there will be a four player multiplayer play mode with cooperative and competitive gameplay.[2]
