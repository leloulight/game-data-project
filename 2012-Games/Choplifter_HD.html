Choplifter HD is a shoot 'em up video game from inXile Entertainment based on Choplifter, which was released on January 10, 2012 for Windows, Xbox 360 and PlayStation 3.

Summary[edit]

In Choplifter HD, players take on the role of an up-and-coming rescue pilot asked to join the elite, international helicopter rescue team C.H.O.P.R. (Coordinated Helicopter Operations, Preservation and Rescue). Pilots are trained to command a variety of helicopters deployed on a variety of different missions, such as captured military prisoner extraction. Players must bank, hover, touch-down and sometimes even shake zombies from their chopper during lift-off in order to make their rescue and complete their mission.[5]

The game is based on Choplifter, an Apple II game from 1985. Dan Gorlin, the creator of the original franchise, served as a design consultant for Choplifter HD.[6]
