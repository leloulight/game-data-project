Demons' Score is a 2012 fantasy rhythm action game developed by iNiS and published by Square Enix for mobile OSs. The gameplay is similar to another iNiS-developed game, Elite Beat Agents.[1] The game was released on iOS on 19 September 2012, and on Android on 27 November 2012.[2]

The game was removed from both iTunes and Google Play in September 2014.[3]

Story[edit]

Serenity is just an average college girl, but when she suddenly loses contact with her father Dr. Aleister, she sets out to find him at the Salem State Hospital & Asylum where he works. Serenity arrives to find she barely recognizes the hospital any more. Stepping warily into the shattered ruins, she meets a talking teddy bear. Introducing himself as David, the talking teddy bear claims to have once been human, but when Serenity asks him about her father’s whereabouts, countless demons suddenly appear. Just before her father disappeared, he had sent a mysterious app to her smartphone. Known as the Demons' Score, the app is a powerful program that enables her to take control of demons that possess her body.[4]
