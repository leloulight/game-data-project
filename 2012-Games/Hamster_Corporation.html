HAMSTER (株式会社ハムスター, Kabushiki-gaisha Hamusutā?) is a Japanese video game publisher, with office located in Setagaya, Tokyo, Japan. The game division of Toshiba-EMI Limited spun off HAMSTER Corporation in November 1999.

In PlayStation Store of Japan, 120 or more titles is distributed to Arcade Archives, and very many titles are sold. HAMSTER Corporation acquired the rights of Nihon Bussan's video games in March 2014.[1]
