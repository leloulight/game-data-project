RollerCoaster Tycoon 3D is a construction and management simulation video game developed by n-Space for the Nintendo 3DS. The game is a spin-off of the RollerCoaster Tycoon series and the first portable game in the franchise.[3]

Reception[edit]

RollerCoaster Tycoon 3D has received mostly negative reviews, with many critics citing that the game does not hold up to its predecessors and has a poorly designed interface. RollerCoaster Tycoon 3D currently has a score of 39 out of 100 on Metacritic.[4]
