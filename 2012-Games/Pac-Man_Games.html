Pac-Man Games

Developer(s) iNPLAS
Publisher(s) Namco Bandai Games
Series Pac-Man
Platform(s) iOS
Release date(s) 29 March 2012 (No longer available)

Pac-Man Games was an iOS application by Namco Bandai Games that contained timed "S" (Score Attack) versions of six different Namco games, with the games being Pac-Man S, Dig Dug S, Galaga S, Rally-X S, Gator Panic S, and Pac-Chain S. It also came with a "My Room" mode where you dress up your avatar and decorate your avatar's room. You used points from the games to unlock more items for your avatar and avatar's room. The application was also linked to Facebook. It was pulled from the App Store on March 30, 2014.
