The Electronic Entertainment Expo 2015, commonly known as E3 2015, is scheduled to be the 21st Electronic Entertainment Expo event. E3 is an annual trade show for the video game industry presented by the Entertainment Software Association (ESA). The event will take place at the Los Angeles Convention Center in Los Angeles, CA from June 16, 2015, to June 18.[1]

Major exhibitors at the convention will include Activision, Bethesda Softworks,[2] Electronic Arts, Microsoft, Sony Computer Entertainment, Nintendo of America, Nvidia, Square Enix[3] and Ubisoft.[4]
