    NASCAR '15 is an upcoming NASCAR video game, the fourth installment in the NASCAR The Game series and an update to the preceding NASCAR '14. Developed by incoming NASCAR licensee DMi games and outgoing licensee Eutechnyx, and published by Deep Silver, it is planned for released on the seventh generation PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 consoles as well as Microsoft Windows. The game will be selling in GameStop with Microsoft Xbox 360 and Sony Playstation 3. Steam will be selling it for Windows PC.

    History[edit]

    On January 1, 2015, HC2 Holdings, Inc. subsidiary DMi Games acquired the NASCAR video game liscence as well as "certain NASCAR-related assets" from Eutechnyx.[2][3] DMi announced that a 2015 update to NASCAR '14 would be released in May for the Xbox 360, PlayStation 3, and Windows PC, with a 2016 version in development for the eight generation consoles.[1][2]
