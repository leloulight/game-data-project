Triad Wars is an upcoming open world online multiplayer video game developed by United Front Games in conjunction with Square Enix London Studios and published by Square Enix.[1] It is a spin-off to the 2012 video game Sleeping Dogs and the second installment in the Sleeping Dogs series. The game will have more traditional MMO elements than Sleeping Dogs, with the central goal being to "rise to power as a criminal kingpin of the Triad underworld."[3]

Development[edit]

In October 2013, United Front Games confirmed that a game, titled Triad Wars, set within Sleeping Dogs‍ '​ universe was in production. The developer confirmed that it would be published by Square Enix,[4] and would be shown to the public in 2014. Triad Wars was described by the developers as "something we've wanted to do for ages but just never had the chance. But now we do."[5]

United Front Games teased that Triad Wars would be a PC online game on 19 September 2014.[6] A reveal took place on 22 September 2014, which included an announcement trailer.[7]
