Milestone S.r.l. is an Italian video game developer with headquarters in Milan. It was founded in 1994 by Antonio Farina as Graffiti, and soon renamed to Milestone in 1996. Best known for the successful Superbike and WRC FIA series, they are the leading developer of interactive entertainment software in Italy.[2]
