I am Bread is an action-adventure simulation game developed and published by Bossa Studios and was released on 9 April 2015. The game is currently available on Windows and OS X.[1] The game has a cartoony feel.

Gameplay[edit]

I am Bread puts the player in control of slices of bread and the aim of each level is to turn the bread into toast. If the bread, before becoming toast, touches objects the "edible" meter will go down. Objects and substances that can make this go down can be water and the floor. To control the bread the player must use the arrow keys or an analog stick.[2] The levels are based on the seven days of the week.

Reception[edit]

This section requires expansion. (April 2015)

PC Gamer writer Jordan Erica Webber gave the game a 58/100 score and said, "more game than you might expect, but still best played for an audience".[2] Kotaku writer Luke Plunkett said, "...this looks like it might actually have some depth beyond just laughing at the bread-walking."[3]

Some YouTubers[who?] have accused I am Bread as being "YouTube bait".[citation needed]
