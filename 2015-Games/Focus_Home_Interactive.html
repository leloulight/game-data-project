Focus Home Interactive is a French independent video game publisher based in Paris, France. Founded in 1996, Focus has published and distributed original titles such as Blood Bowl, Sherlock Holmes, TrackMania, Runaway and sports games like Cycling Manager and Virtual Skipper.

On June 25, 2010 it was announced that Focus had acquired the Cities XL franchise from now defunct Monte Cristo.[1]
