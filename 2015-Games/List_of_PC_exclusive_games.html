Often in the gaming industry, video games are released as exclusives to single platforms due to hardware, marketing, monetary constraints, and even paid exclusivity. The following is a list and table of video games created exclusively for the PC platform while maintaining exclusivity for at least a year. It includes games for multiple operating systems, such as Windows, Linux, DOS, Unix, and OSX. Although some older operating systems may no longer be available in retail machines, they retain playability through emulation and virtual machines, and are very capable of playing games from this list.
