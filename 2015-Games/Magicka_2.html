Magicka 2, the successor of Magicka,[2] is an upcoming video game for PlayStation 4 and Windows. It has a planned release date of May 26, 2015 [3] and was announced at Sony's E3 2014 media briefing with a trailer.[2] Instead of being developed by Arrowhead Game Studios, the title is being developed by Pieces Interactive[4] alongside the publisher Paradox Interactive.
