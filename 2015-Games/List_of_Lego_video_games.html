List of Lego video games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Since 1997, 56 video games based on the Lego construction system have been released. Originally, games were based exclusively on Lego properties. In 2001, Lego Creator: Harry Potter introduced games based on licensed, non-Lego intellectual properties. Since then, Lego has licensed several other properties for use in its video games, including Star Wars, Batman and Rock Band.

Licensed properties[edit]
