MotoGP 15 is a video game developed by Milestone S.r.l..[1] The game is set to be released for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, PlayStation Vita, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One in June 2015.[2][3]

Features[edit]

This section requires expansion. (March 2015)

Moto GP 15 will Features the Official Riders from the 2015 & 2014 season of the Moto 3 Moto 2 & Moto GP and it will also feature customisable bikes
