Ratchet & Clank is an upcoming platformer video game under development by Insomniac Games, and to be published by Sony Computer Entertainment. The game is planned to be released on the PlayStation 4 in 2015. It is a "re-imagining" of the first game in the series, based on the upcoming movie by Rainmaker Entertainment and Blockade Entertainment.[2]

Development[edit]

Ratchet & Clank was announced during Sony's press conference at E3 2014. Insomniac Games' California and North Carolina studios are cooperating on its development.[3] Developers of the first game are stated to be working on the game, such as long time design director Brian Allgeier.
