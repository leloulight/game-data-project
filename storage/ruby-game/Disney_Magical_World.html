Disney Magical World (ディズニー マジックキャッスル マイ・ハッピー・ライフ, Deizunī Majikku Shiro: No Shiawasena Seikatsu?, Disney Magic Castle: My Happy Life)[3] is a life simulation video game for the Nintendo 3DS. The game was released in Japan on August 1, 2013 and April 11, 2014 in North America.[3][4]

Gameplay[edit]

The Player and The Cafe

The game starts off with your character that you made or using a Mii as your character. The player can go on quests, use materials for furniture, wallpaper, clothing, food, drinks and snacks. You can also edit your cafe, do more to earn stickers to unlock more areas, more clothing, furniture or to make future Disney Characters and help them get what they need which will cause them to reward you.

Reception[edit]

Disney Magical World has received mostly positive reviews, scoring 81/100 on Metacritic[5] and 77.83% on GameRankings.[6] Nintendo World Report awarded the game 8.5/10, praising the wide range of activities and gameplay but felt that the loading times interrupted the game's progress.[7] Game Revolution gave it a 3 out of 5, saying while packed with characters and collectables, criticized the repetitive tasks and lack of a main attraction.[8]

As of 31 March 2014, the game has shipped 500,000 copies.[9]
