Pushmo World (known in Europe as Pullblox World, and in Japan as Hikuosu World (引ク押ス ワールド?)) is a puzzle game developed by Intelligent Systems and published by Nintendo for the Wii U video game console. The game is a sequel to Pushmo and Crashmo, and was released worldwide on June 19, 2014.

Gameplay[edit]

As in previous games, players can choose from a selection of 250 puzzles or create their own puzzles and share them with others.

Development[edit]

The game was first announced May 28, 2014 for Wii U.[1][2]

Reception[edit]

Pushmo World received generally positive reviews, scoring 82/100 on Metacritic and 81% on GameRankings.[3][4]
