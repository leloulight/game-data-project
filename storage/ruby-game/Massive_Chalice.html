Massive Chalice is an upcoming turn-based strategy video game for Xbox One, Microsoft Windows, OS X, and Linux by Double Fine Productions, which was funded through Kickstarter.[3] The project leader of the game is Brad Muir,[4] who was previously the project leader of Iron Brigade.[5] It was expected to be released in September 2014 until the game was delayed.[3]

Development[edit]

In May 2013, Massive Chalice became the focus of Double Fine's second Kickstarter campaign, after Broken Age (formerly known as Double Fine Adventure) in 2012.[6] The Double Fine team had learned from their mistakes on the first kickstarter and opted to not show as much exclusive material to backers, as this created a rift between the backers and non-backers, as well as members of the press.[7] Kickstarter backers influenced the development of the game when the ability for same sex marriage was added to the game after a number of requests.[8] Brad Muir stated that backer input before development was one of the advantages of Kickstarter, since a feature such as this might not have been thought about until the game was almost completed, making it harder to implement. He also went on to state that if the game was made under a traditional publisher agreement, the publisher might not have allowed the feature to be added to the game in order to avoid controversy.[9]
