Pac-Man and the Ghostly Adventures 2 is a 2014 video game for the Wii U, Nintendo 3DS, Xbox 360 and PlayStation 3, released on October 14, 2014. It is the sequel to Pac-Man and the Ghostly Adventures

Development[edit]

The game was first announced April 22, 2014.[2]

Gameplay[edit]

Just like the original game, Pac-Man and the Ghostly Adventures 2, uses the same features as its predecessor.

Pac-Man and the Ghostly Adventures 2 features new story lines, characters and worlds.
