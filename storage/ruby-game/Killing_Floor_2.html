Killing Floor 2
Developer(s) Tripwire Interactive
Engine Unreal Engine 3
Platform(s) Microsoft Windows,[1] SteamOS[1]
Release date(s) TBA
Mode(s) Single-player, multiplayer

Killing Floor 2 is an upcoming video game developed by Tripwire Interactive. It is a sequel to the original Killing Floor, which was originally released as an Unreal Tournament 2004 mod in 2005
