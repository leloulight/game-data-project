NBA Live 15 is an upcoming basketball video game for the PlayStation 4 and Xbox One releasing on October 28, 2014 in North America and October 31, 2014 in Europe. NBA Live 15 features Damian Lillard of the Portland Trail Blazers as its cover athlete. The release date was moved due to EA Sports wanting to polish the title before release.[1]

Gameplay[edit]

The first gameplay look is introduced by EA Sports with 8 player 3D-scans. The changes is a graphical improvements for the jerseys, arenas, shot clocks, the basket, and more.[2] EA Sports scanned 70% of NBA players. Other changes will be announced soon with gameplay improvements.
