The 10th British Academy Video Game Awards awarded by the British Academy of Film and Television Arts, is an award ceremony that was held on 12 March 2014 in the Tobacco Dock. The ceremony honoured achievement in video gaming in 2013 and was hosted by Dara Ó Briain.

Multiple nominations and wins[edit]

Wins[edit]

  • 5 wins - The Last of Us
  • 3 wins - Grand Theft Auto V and Tearaway

Nominations[edit]

  • 10 nominations - The Last of Us
  • 9 nominations - Grand Theft Auto V
  • 8 nominations - Tearaway
  • 4 nominations - Assassin’s Creed IV: Black Flag, BioShock Infinite, Papers, Please, Super Mario 3D World, and The Stanley Parable
  • 3 nominations - Badland, Beyond: Two Souls, Brothers: A Tale of Two Sons, Device 6, and Tomb Raider
  • 2 nominations - Battlefield 4, Forza Motorsport 5, Gone Home, Gunpoint, Lego Marvel Super Heroes, Ni No Kuni: Wrath of the White Witch, and The Room Two
