Sid Meier's Civilization Revolution 2 is a 2014 iteration of Civilization and sequel to Civilization Revolution developed by Firaxis Games with Sid Meier as designer for the iOS.[1]

Gameplay[edit]

The game will feature returning leaders from the original Civilization Revolution, such as Abraham Lincoln and Napoleon Bonaparte, along with new leaders such as Winston Churchill and John F. Kennedy.

Releases[edit]

The iOS version of the game was released in July 2014.[2] A forthcoming android version has been announced, though specific dates have not been set.[3]

Reception[edit]

The game was moderately well received with a score of 66 on Metacritic. In comparison to other games released however, this places Civilization Revolution 2 in the bottom quarter of games scored on Metacritic for the iOS platform in 2014.[4]
