Turn 10 Studios is a video game development company from the United States. It was established in 2001 by Microsoft Studios to develop the Forza Motorsport series for the Xbox, Xbox 360 and Xbox One consoles.[1]

Studio structure[edit]

Turn 10 is made up of over 300 employees as of the release of Forza Motorsport 3 in 2009.
