Galaga Legions DX is a downloadable game that's part of the Galaga series. It is a follow-up to the 2008 game, Galaga Legions, and the second game with the Namco Generations label. The game was released for Xbox Live Arcade on June 29, 2011 and PlayStation Network on August 3, 2011.
