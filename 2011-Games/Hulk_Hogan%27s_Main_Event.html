Hulk Hogan's Main Event is a professional wrestling fighting game starring Hulk Hogan created by Panic Button and published by Majesco Entertainment for the Xbox 360. The game requires the Kinect peripheral.[1]

Reception[edit]

Reception
Review scores
Publication Score
Game Informer 1/10[3]
Aggregate scores
Metacritic 26/100[2]

Hulk Hogan's Main Event has been panned by critics. Dan Ryckert of Game Informer gave the game a score of 1 out of 10, commenting that "Kinect has a hard time recognizing many required motions, and it's nowhere near fun even when it's working perfectly."[3] Dave Rudden of Official Xbox Magazine gave the game a 2 out of 10, saying that "the career mode is a bore, the cartoony visual style is ugly, and the voice implementation is super-sloppy."[4] GamesRadar ranked it as the 40th worst game ever made. The staff criticized its poor Kinect implementation and the dated use of Hulk Hogan.[5]
