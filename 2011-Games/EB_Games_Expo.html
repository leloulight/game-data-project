EB Games Expo, commonly known as EB Expo or EBX, is a video game trade fair / convention held annually in Australia. It is organised by EB Games Australia. It is used by many video game developers to show off their upcoming games and game-related hardware. Unlike E3, EB Expo allows members of the general public to attend. EB Expo is Australia's largest games event with over 38,000 attending the show in 2013.[1] The most recent show was held in 2014 from October 3 to 5.[2][3]

EB Games Expo 2011[edit]

EB Games Expo 2011 was held at the Gold Coast Convention and Exhibition Centre from the 15-16 of October it was the first time the event was open to the public. The expo was attended by 14,278 people and 26 exhibitors.

EB Games Expo 2012[edit]

Logo For EB Expo 2012

EB Games Expo 2012 was held at the Sydney Showground from the 5-7 of October, and was the first time it was held at the venue. It was announced during the Friday and Saturday 'Twilight Spectacular' shows that the Expo will be held at the same location in 2013 and 2014, as part of a 3-year contract for the event organiser. The expo was attended by 30,745 people and 32 exhibitors.[4]
