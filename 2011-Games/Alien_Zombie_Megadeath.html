Alien Zombie Megadeath is a 2011 action-shooter game, developed by PomPom Games for the Windows and PlayStation 3.

The game features alien creatures, such as bats, amoebas, blobs and zombie critters. These creatures spawn at various levels of the game for soul-purpose of killing the character.

The game is the sequel to the 2010 video game Alien Zombie Death.[4]
