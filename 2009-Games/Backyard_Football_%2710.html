Backyard Football '10 is a sports game released for PlayStation 2, Wii and Xbox 360. It was developed by Humongous Entertainment and published by Atari. This is the tenth iteration of the Backyard Football games from the Backyard Sports series. It was released on October 20, 2009 and is the first to be released on Xbox 360, though not being released on PC or Nintendo DS unlike the previous two installments.

Gameplay[edit]

Players take control of a collection of fictional kids and pint-sized versions of real-life National Football League (NFL) players like Adrian Peterson, Eli Manning, and Maurice Jones-Drew. Even though the players look like children, the action is straight out of the NFL with realistic strategies and playbooks. Backyard Football also features power-ups, custom teams, and unlockable items designed to give the game an arcade feel. The game features "Single Player," "Season," "Tournament," and "All Pro" modes, along with the multiplayer modes of co-op play and two-on-two contests.[2]

Reception[edit]

The game received mixed reviews for the Xbox 360 and Wii versions, but negative reviews for the PS2 version.[3][4][5] For the Xbox 360 version, ZTGameDomain said that the game is "simple, easy to pick up and really well designed".[6]
