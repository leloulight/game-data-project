Dokapon Journey, known in Japan as Dokapon Journey! Nakayoku Kenka Shite♪ (ドカポンジャーニー! 〜なかよくケンカしてっ♪〜?), is a 2008 RPG/board game developed by Suzak and published by Sting in Japan for the Nintendo DS on July 31, 2008. It was later published by Atlus in North America on April 14, 2009.
