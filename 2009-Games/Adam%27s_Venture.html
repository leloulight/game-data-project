Adam's Venture is an episodic Christian adventure game developed by Vertigo Games, a Dutch indie developer.[1] The Adam's Venture trilogy is available within the United States from GraceWorks Interactive's Christian Games NOW. [2]

Series[edit]

  • Episode 1: "The Search for the Lost Garden", was released in June 2009 (Europe), Worldwide release: Q1 2010.
  • Episode 2: "Solomon's Secret" was announced in January 2010 and released on April 8, 2011
  • Episode 3: "Revelations" was released on March 9, 2012.

Story[edit]

The player directs the young explorer Adam Venture on a quest for the Garden of Eden and Solomon's Temple

Reception[edit]

Episode 1 received average reviews from critics, garnering a 52/100 on the review aggregation website Metacritic.[3]
