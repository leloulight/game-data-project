Boing! Docomodake DS (ぽろろんっ! ドコモダケDS, Pororon! Docomodake DS?) is a puzzle game/platformer game starring NTT DoCoMo's mascot, Docomodake. Their rival is NHK's mascot, Domo-Kun who has been in games of his own. It was developed by Suzak and AQ Interactive.
